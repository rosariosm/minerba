WE ARE DIFFERENT

OUR COMPANY

We are a corporate communication agency, based in Buenos Aires, Argentina, operating locally in Chile and Uruguay.

2013

LAUNCH

We were born as PSM Corporate Communication, the first Argentine agency to work with the concept of Corporate Communication. With a 100% bilingual team, we were the first to offer services from Argentina to Uruguay and Chile with the logistic support of local partners.
2014

CONSOLIDATION

Clients joined us one by one and as our bonds grow stronger each year, they keep choosing us and growing in trust and services.
2018

NEW SERVICES

The incorporation of new partners allows us to provide new services, disruptive to the traditional market of communication agencies. We redefine and differentiate our company with strength, experience, and knowledge about the problems and solutions from the perspective of those who know from first hand about the decision-making processes.
2018

PROJECTION

After several proposals, we decided to work with the very best of Europe, the most advanced and innovative, those who know how to do more and better. For that reason, we became the South American partners of Prodigioso Volcán, one of the fastest growing companies in the EU according to the Financial Times (2018).

NEW NAME

Today, as an experienced and recognized agency, we grow in services and equipment. We chose to call ourselves MINERBA Corporate Communication, after the Roman goddess of intelligence and strategy,Minerva; with B for Buenos Aires, our operational center. or with BA, for the initials of Buenos Aires, our operational center.

WHO WE ARE

Patricia Santa Marina

Founder/CEO

Political Scientist and Ph.D. candidate in Sociology, she specialized professionally in international PR and Corporate Communication. She has worked with media leaders from Argentina and the world in both media companies and multinationals. She has made an axis in the practice of Corporate Communication, defense of industry, freedom of the press, positioning for companies and brands and media construction. With more than 15 years of experience in management positions, she has led communication teams for the entire Latin American region. In 2013, she created PSM Corporate Communication - today MINERBA Corporate Communication - with the support of first-line professionals with varied experience and training, both nationally and internationally.

Javier Ghía

Socio

Javier is a lawyer and an MBA (ESEADE) with several years at C level positions in multinational media companies. Javier later created two successful advertising agencies. Now he is a special advisor for international boards in matters regarding new business models and cultural change in organizations.

Astrid Walberg

Account Manager

Astrid studied Political Science at the Universidad Católica Argentina (UCA), and majored in Marketing Communications. After several years of working in multinational companies, she joined our agency in 2015.

Valeria Parodi

Finance & Administration Manager

Sofía Duggan

Account Manager

Natalia Biados

International Associate Consultant
