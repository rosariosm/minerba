OUR PROCESSES

We conceive our work as a comprehensive solution focusing on three fundamental stages.

DIAGNOSIS

Being results oriented, we produce an in-depth analysis of the initial situation, based on the client's goals. In this way, we are able to evaluate the success chances of a communicational improvement plan.

PLANNING

We design and generate the necessary steps, actions and content to successfully fulfill the client's objectives.

ACTING

Based on the proposals developed, we optimize resources to obtain actual results.

WE ARE DIFFERENT

OUR COMPANY

We are a Corporate Communication Agency, based in Buenos Aires, Argentina, operating locally in Chile and Uruguay..

2013

LAUNCH

We started off as PSM Corporate Communication, the first Argentine agency to work with the concept of Corporate Communication. With a 100% bilingual team, we were also the first local agency to offer services from Argentina to LATAM, USA, and Europe, with logistic support from local professional associates.
2014

CONSOLIDATION

Clients have progressively joined us and keep us as their top choice over the years while trusting us with even more services.
2018

NEW SERVICES

The incorporation of associates allowed us to provide new services, disruptive to the traditional market of communication agencies. We have redefined and differentiated our company with sound experience, and a deep understanding of current and future challenges.

INTERNATIONAL ALLIANCE

We wanted to work along with top-level international professionals to further expand our work. That is why we are now proud partners for South America of Prodigioso Volcán, widely considered the most visionary and innovative agency in Europe and one of the fastest growing companies in the EU, according to the Financial Times (2018).

NEW NAME

Today, as an experienced and recognized agency, we are at the verge of a new era, and choose to call ourselves MINERBA Corporate Communication, after the Roman goddess of intelligence and strategy, Minerva; but with a “B”, to include “BA” in our name after Buenos Aires, home to our headquarters.

Patricia Santa Marina

Founder & CEO

Political Scientist and Ph.D. candidate in Sociology, she specialized in international PR and Corporate Communication. Patricia has worked with media leaders from Argentina and the world in both media companies and multinationals. She has made an axis in media as a business, freedom of the press, the positioning of companies, brands and start-ups and has experience in the development of media from square one. With more than 15 years of experience in C-level positions, she has led communication teams for the entire Latin American region. In 2013, she founded PSM Corporate Communication, today MINERBA Corporate Communication.

Javier Ghía

Socio

Javier is a lawyer from Universidad de Buenos Aires (UBA) and completed an MBA in the ESADE Business School of Barcelona. He worked for prestigious international banks, led the start-up of a leading NGO and the Bloomberg Media Business in Latin America. He was also an invited Professor at FLACSO and a lecturer on topics of innovation and cultural change. Entrepreneur in business and social projects, Javier worked in digital transformation processes as a consultant for frontline companies.
